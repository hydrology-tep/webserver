Satellite Acquisitions

EO missions providing data contributions to the CEOS Seismic Hazards and Volcano Pilots are listed below. The contribution can be in the form of specific data acquisition campaigns to make sure appropriate observations will be available over the pilot areas or access to data for generating EO based measurements. Each CEOS member providing a contribution concerning an EO mission is responsible for data access. Furthermore space agencies are providing access to EO data collections in the framework of the GSNL initiative. The platform geobrowser allows to display relevant collections to support the geohazards community.
